About

Dr. Erlich is a boutique consultancy working at the intersection of engineering, product strategy, and market communication. We build narratives and go-to-market systems that engineers respect and buyers understand.

Technical depth. Commercial clarity.

Many B2B tech firms struggle to explain value without oversimplifying. We bridge that gap by translating technical differentiation into positioning, messaging, and sales enablement.

Typical engagements include strategy sprints, product positioning workshops, website/message rewrites, and roadmap-to-market alignment.

  • Outcome-driven: messaging frameworks, ICP definitions, and GTM plans you can execute
  • Engineer-friendly: we respect constraints, performance realities, and system context
  • Fast cycles: structured workshops → drafts → test → iterate
